About this Creator

Welcome to the offline Dragonbane Character Creator. This tool guides you step-by-step through generating a character strictly adhering to the core rules.

How to Use

  • Step 1: Basics. Choose your Kin, Profession, and Age. Mages must select a magic school. You can manually select options or use the dice roll buttons.
  • Step 2: Attributes. Roll 4D6 (dropping the lowest) for your base attributes. The app automatically calculates your final score based on your selected Age.
  • Step 3: Background. Determine your Appearance, Weakness, Memento, and starting Gear.
  • Step 4: Skills. You receive a pool of points based on your Age. You must pick at least 6 skills from your Profession's allowed list. Mages also select their starting Spells and Tricks here.
  • Step 5: Final Sheet. Review your fully calculated digital character sheet. From here, you can print the page or save it as a highly-compact PDF.

This tool was created with the assistance of AI.
